## Risks and Mitigations

### Risk 1: Adoption Resistance
**Symptom:** Developers ignore glossary, suppress checks  
**Likelihood:** Medium  
**Mitigation:**
- Default advisory enforcement (not blocking)
- Demonstrate value in onboarding sessions
- Make Contextive optional, not mandatory
- Celebrate early wins publicly

### Risk 2: Maintenance Burden
**Symptom:** Glossary becomes stale, diverges from reality  
**Likelihood:** High  
**Mitigation:**
- Mandatory ownership per bounded context
- Quarterly review cadence (scheduled)
- Staleness detection automation
- Clear escalation path for conflicts

### Risk 3: Over-Engineering
**Symptom:** Too many terms, excessive categorization, complex tooling  
**Likelihood:** Low  
**Mitigation:**
- Start with 50-70 core terms only
- Expand incrementally based on need
- Keep Contextive setup simple
- Measure adoption, not coverage
