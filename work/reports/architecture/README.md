## Report Structure

Architecture reports follow this structure:

1. **Executive Summary** - Quick overview for decision-makers

## Review Process

1. **Request Review** - Manager Mike or team lead requests architectural review
2. **Conduct Review** - Architect Alphonso examines code, tests, documentation, ADR compliance
3. **Document Findings** - Create executive summary, full review, action items
4. **Go/No-Go Decision** - Clear verdict with confidence level
5. **Distribute Reports** - Share with stakeholders and team
6. **Track Actions** - Monitor completion of action items
