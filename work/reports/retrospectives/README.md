# Retrospectives

Sprint retrospectives, process reviews, and lessons learned from development cycles.

## Purpose

Retrospectives capture what worked, what didn't, and what to improve in future iterations. They provide a record of team learning and continuous improvement.

## Content Guidelines

- One retrospective per sprint or major milestone
- Focus on process, collaboration, and team dynamics
- Document specific action items and improvements
- Include metrics and observations
- Reference related work artifacts when relevant

## Organization

Retrospectives follow the naming convention:
`YYYY-MM-DD-<sprint-or-milestone>-retrospective.md`
