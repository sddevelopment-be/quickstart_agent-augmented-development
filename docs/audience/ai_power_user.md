# Persona: AI/LLM Power User (Consistency-Seeking)

**Category:** `EXTERNAL Stakeholder`  
**Audience Type:** End user, advanced prompt engineer, or process optimizer who relies on predictable, customizable LLM/AI assistant behavior.  
**Primary Goal:** To achieve consistent, reliable, and repeatable agent/assistant experiences—reducing forgetfulness or _“identity drift”_.  
**Reading Context:** Consulted in user documentation, persona-relevant tutorials, and when framing use cases in adoption guides or community onboarding.  

---

## Overview

> **Purpose:**   
> An individual (technical or not) aiming to maintain the identity, memory, and “character” of their AI assistant or chatbot across sessions and tasks.

* **Role Focus:**
  Prompt engineer, power user, workflow optimizer.

* **Primary Function:**
  Adopts structures, directives, and memory aids from the repo to reduce forgetting, identity drift, or unpredictable assistant behavior.

* **Environment / Context:**
  Solo practitioners, knowledge workers, AI-driven teams, automation enthusiasts.

---

## Core Motivations

* **Professional Drivers:** Reliable, context-aware assistant behavior.
* **Emotional or Cognitive Drivers:** Frustration with LLM/AI “forgetfulness” or role confusion; values continuity and control.
* **Systemic Positioning:** End user, sometimes evangelist or prompt-sharing community member.

---

| Category    | Expectation / Need | Description                                   |

| Information | Role templates     | Easy-to-load identity and guideline scripts   |

| Governance  | Trust guarantees  | Controls against “going rogue” or changing tone|

---

## Frustrations and Constraints

* **Pain Points:** AI assistants forgetting context, inconsistent behavior, session resets.
* **Trade-Off Awareness:** Tolerates complexity in setup if it leads to reliable outcome.
* **Environmental Constraints:** Limits of AI model context window, platform quirks, chat session timeouts.

---

## Behavioral Cues

| Situation            | Typical Behavior            | Interpretation   |
|----------------------|----------------------------|------------------|
| Stable / Routine     | Follows scripts, reloads role | Likes predictability|
| Change / Uncertainty | Reapplies templates, seeks fixes | Persists until reliable |
| Under Pressure       | Escalates, seeks human support| Needs fast recovery   |

* **Feedback Expectations:** “It just works,” succinct, reproducible steps.
