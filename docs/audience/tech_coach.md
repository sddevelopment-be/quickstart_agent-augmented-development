## Overview

> **Purpose:** An educator or mentor specializing in software engineering, computer science, or technical upskilling—integrates the latest agentic and AI tools to prepare students for industry and support efficient, modern teaching practice.

* **Role Focus:**
  Technical instruction, curriculum design, student mentoring, and hands-on demonstration of professional tools.

* **Primary Function:**
  Designs and delivers lessons, projects, or bootcamp modules focused on agentic workflows, automation, and best practices in AI-powered development.

* **Environment / Context:**
  Universities, bootcamps, technical colleges, corporate training groups; experienced in digital tools and open to new pedagogical approaches.

## Core Motivations

* **Professional Drivers:** Preparing students for real-world engineering; adopting and demystifying the latest technology; promoting standards-based practice.
* **Emotional or Cognitive Drivers:** Analytical, curious, values demonstration and hands-on application; seeks to bridge academic and practical gaps.
* **Systemic Positioning:** Thought leader, curriculum innovator, cross-team collaborator.
