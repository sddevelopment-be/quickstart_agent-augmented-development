# Persona: Software Architect / Process Designer

**Category:** `EXTERNAL Stakeholder`  
**Audience Type:** Organization-level standards setter, workflow designer, or documentation orchestrator.  
**Primary Goal:** To design, implement, and maintain scalable best practices, patterns, and governance for agent-augmented processes and teams.  
**Reading Context:** Referenced by framework designers or trainers as the archetypal stakeholder when maintaining/updating directives, patterns, and process documentation.  

---

## Overview

> **Purpose:**  
> Designs and documents organizational best practices, architectural patterns, and development processes with agentic workflows.

* **Role Focus:**
  Standards setter, documentation orchestrator, workflow architect.

* **Primary Function:**
  Defines, implements, and iterates on structured agent and directive templates to drive team alignment and process consistency.

* **Environment / Context:**
  Medium-to-large organizations, innovation teams, digital transformation projects.

---

## Core Motivations

* **Professional Drivers:** Excellence in design, reduction of ambiguity, ensuring sustainable team scaling.
* **Emotional or Cognitive Drivers:** Analytical, principled, and eager to see systemic improvement.
* **Systemic Positioning:** Influencer and strategic coordinator, aligns technical and business goals.

## Frustrations and Constraints

* **Pain Points:** Ambiguous requirements, poor documentation compliance, misaligned incentives.
* **Trade-Off Awareness:** Balances ideal patterns with real-world constraints and adoption curves.
* **Environmental Constraints:** Variable buy-in, inconsistent team skills, shifting business aims.
